About me.

Hi, I'm Kenyatta. I'm a UX designer in North Carolina who loves telling stories, solving problems, and creating delightful experiences that connect people with the information they need.
I have a background in librarianship that's made me develop a passion for ensuring information gets into the hands of those who need it. This has influenced my goal to design solutions that are useful, inclusive, and a joy to use.
When I'm not working on design problems, you can find me reading, painting, or feeding the neighborhood crows.
